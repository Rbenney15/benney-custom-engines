About Us

Benney's Custom Engines has been locally owned and operated since 1987. We provide high quality service and repairs for high performance needs. We strive to meet and exceed customers expectations, we also initiate innovative ways to improve social, economical and moral responsibility.

Services

Services we provide:

  • Precision engine machining and computerized balancing
  • Engine rebuilding, repair, and design new projects
  • Dyno testing and tuning
  • EFI conversions and repairs
  • Powertrain restoration of classic & muscle cars
  • General classic and muscle car repairs
  • High horsepower pump gas engines
  • Grudge Race engines (We are discrete with our builds if you are in the Grudge Race Community)
  • Monthly storage of classic and muscle cars in a clean and dry environment
